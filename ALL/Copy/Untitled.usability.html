  1. Method:
    1. Website Amazon - : https://www.amazon.com/ref=nav_logo
    2. Target audience: anyone who has access to the internet
    3. Three representative tasks for this website:
      • Find a product that is very rare and common to find in-store or online.
      • Proceed to check-out and then cancel it.
      • Then call a customer service representative.
  2. Results: It took the subjects about 10 mins to carry out the entire method. It took about 8 steps to complete the tasks.
  3. Discussion: The current design is effective but it can be designed differently so that users could more easily perform the representative tasks. For example, adding a small customer service call button would help someone to reduce the need to search for a customer service number.
